About Protect abandoned dogs

The number of abandoned dogs is steadily increasing. It has increased by 1.5 times compared to last year. There are many reasons, but most of them are abandoned because they are no longer cute or are a financial burden. We are trying to protect abandoned dogs so that this does not happen anymore. According to the current reviews, everyone is satisfied.
